(let us tremble)a personal radiance sits hideously upon the trafficking hum of dusk each street takes of shadowy light the drollsnowing delirium (we do not speak!) tumbled hushingly bits of downward flower flowing without or cease or time;a naming stealth of ectasy means,like a girl lasciviously frail, peace (dreaming is better) murdering coolness slowly in peopling places seeks play:withs of star link clauses of warmth (after dream who knows?) a blackish cat and a bluish cat are eyeing,as with almost melancholy delicacy night gargles windows.
