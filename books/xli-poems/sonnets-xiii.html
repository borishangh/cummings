when i am in Boston,i do not speak. and i sit in the click of ivory balls.... noting flies,which jerk upon the weak colour of table-cloths,the electric When In Doubt Buy Of(but a roof hugs whom) as the august evening mauls Kneeland,and a waiter cleverly lugs indigestible honeycake to men ....one perfectly smooth coffee tasting of hellas,i drink,or sometimes two remarking cries of paklavah meeah.
