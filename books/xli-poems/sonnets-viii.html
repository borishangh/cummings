[]
come nothing to my comparable soul which with existence has conversed in vain, O scrupulously take thy trivial toll, for whose cool feet this frantic heart is fain; try me with thy perfumes which have seduced the mightier nostrils of the fervent dead, feed with felicities me wormperused by whom the hungering mouth of time is fed: and if i like not what thou givest me to him let me complain,whose seat is where revolving planets struggle to be free with the astounding everlasting air— but if i like,i’ll take between thy hands what no man feels,no woman understands.
