if being morticed with a dream myself speaks (whispering, suggesting that our souls inhabit whatever is between them) knowing my lips hands the way i move my habits laughter i say you will perhaps pardon, possibly you will comprehend. and how this has arrived your mind may guess if at sunset it should,leaning against me,smile; or(between dawn and twilight)giving your eyes,present me also with the terror of shrines which noone has suspected(but wherein silently always are kneeling the various deaths which are your lover lady:together with what keen innumerable lives he has not lived.z
